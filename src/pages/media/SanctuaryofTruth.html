The Sanctuary of Truth Museum is a magnificent wooden architectural masterpiece located by the seaside in Naklua, Pattaya, Thailand. It was created by Thai businessman Lek Viriyaphant and serves as both a temple and a museum that celebrates philosophy, art, and spirituality. The entire structure is made from hand-carved teak wood and stands over 100 meters tall, filled with beautiful sculptures inspired by Buddhist and Hindu beliefs. Each carving inside the museum tells a meaningful story about life, morality, and the harmony between humans, nature, and the universe.
