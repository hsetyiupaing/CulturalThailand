Wat Rong Khun, also known as the White Temple, is a contemporary, unconventional Buddhist temple in Chiang Rai, Thailand. Designed by Thai artist Chalermchai Kositpipat, the temple is famous for its striking white color symbolizing purity and its intricate glass mosaics that reflect the teachings of Buddha. The architecture blends traditional Thai style with surreal, modern elements, making it one of the most unique temples in the world.
