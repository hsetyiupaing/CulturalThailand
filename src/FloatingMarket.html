Welcome to Damnoen Saduak Floating Market

Opening Hour

Daily from 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Location

Damnoen Saduak District, Ratchaburi Province, Thailand (About 100 kilometers southwest of Bangkok)

Price

Visiting the market is free. Boat rides cost 200 to 400 Baht per person depending on duration.

The Damnoen Saduak Floating Market is one of the most famous and vibrant markets in Ratchaburi Province, Thailand. It offers a lively glimpse into traditional Thai river life, where local vendors paddle wooden boats filled with fresh fruits, vegetables, handmade goods, and delicious Thai street food. The market reflects Thailandâ€™s long-standing relationship with water and showcases the charm of daily life along the canals. Visitors can take a boat ride through the narrow waterways, interact with friendly local sellers, and experience the colorful sights, sounds, and aromas of this unique marketplace. It is also a wonderful place to taste authentic Thai snacks, shop for souvenirs, and capture beautiful photographs of the bustling atmosphere. The market is most active in the morning when the canals are filled with boats and activity.
